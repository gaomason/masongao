Reciprocity 12/5/24

I have very, very high expectations of my friends. Love loyalty. I think that this is because I feel like I would be willing to anything for them. So, I expect them to be willing and wanting to do anything for me. I set my expectations high, so that when they are not met, I end up hurt. It’s a vicious cycle. But, I think that that is just who I am. I don’t want to be friends with those that don’t meet my expectations because I know that our friendship will be on unequal footing. I don’t want to be willing and wanting to do anything for someone who wouldn’t do the same for me. Which is also a reason as to why I dislike passivity.
