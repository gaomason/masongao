Disappointment 12/5/24

Our friend group of 6 has been splintered. Austin decided that he hates me, and started to act really resentful towards me. Whenever everyone was together, and I was there, he would just ruin the vibe. In the early days, I could tell that he was trying to exclude me, so I tried extra hard to become better friends with everyone else. And, that worked. I would just act normal, and allow him to destroy himself.

However, he has started to try to actively attack me, to exclude me. He tried to invite everyone in the group, besides me, to dinner. Was not going to let that slide. I was outraged. Have not seen this scale of 人生攻击since middle school.

But, I am more disappointed in the guys in the group. I feel that they should be defending me. In my opinion, there is a very clear right and wrong in this situation. I’m the victim here. Yet, they are trying to excuse his actions. They acknowledge that I ‘ve done nothing, yet they still want me to bow to his irrational demands. Honestly, I feel so disappointed in them. I feel that if my two friends are beefing, and there is a clear right and wrong, you have a responsibility to tell the wrong one to stop. They are still your friend after all. But some people are just like that. Passive. They don’t have the backbone to criticize a friend, when criticism is probably the most valuable thing a friend can offer.

I can’t be good friends with them. Or passive people in general. Cowards. I need to be able to trust that my friends have my back, especially when I am quite clearly in the white. I cannot see grey, especially if grey involves having to hurt myself. Ultimately, they made the decision to hurt me. I can’t be friends with people who would knowingly hurt their friends so that they can stand safely on the sidelines. I guess I value loyalty. A lot.
