I would just act normal, and allow him to destroy himself.

However, he has started to try to actively attack me, to exclude me. He tried to invite everyone in the group, besides me, to dinner. Was not going to let that slide. I was outraged. Have not seen this scale of 人生攻击since middle school.

I feel that if my two friends are beefing, and there is a clear right and wrong, you have a responsibility to tell the wrong one to stop. They are still your friend after all. But some people are just like that. Passive. They don’t have the backbone to criticize a friend, when criticism is probably the most valuable thing a friend can offer.

I can’t be good friends with them. Or passive people in general. Cowards. I need to be able to trust that my friends have my back, especially when I am quite clearly in the white. I cannot see grey, especially if grey involves having to hurt myself. Ultimately, they made the decision to hurt me.
