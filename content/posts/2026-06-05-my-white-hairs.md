I just got a haircut, been overdue, last time I got it cut was the last week of march. The haircut was fine. I was just looking at myself in the mirror - I have a lot of new white hairs. Like at least 20. That's really funny.. I always get white hairs after a stressful period, a stressful semester. But never this many, usually 5-10. I guess that's just how it's been. Eye bags, eye lines, also more noticable, though I've been sleeping well. Just getting older. Living.
