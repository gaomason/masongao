title: For the sake of writing
date: 2026-06-28
---

Just returned from my grand trip. I think I was half expecting to have some great epiphany while traveling, and that when I returned, I would be a changed person. But a while ago, I realized that wasn't really going to happen.

Not much to write about. Was fun. Saw lots of beautiful things. Encountered some interesting company. Overall, I feel tired, mostly glad to be back in Shanghai. 

I guess, one thing I mentioned was feeling more at peace. And now that I'm back in a familiar environment, I think that will be something I will need to actively work on as well. Letting go of minor momentary irritation has been almost, involuntary, natural. But letting go of any types of old wounds and bitterness, especially if they pop up right into your face - That will be much more difficult, and a true test of where this monk mentality may lead me.
