---
title: Slowing down
date: 2026-06-01
---

Been a busy few days, I have been showing a friend around Shanghai, going to all the tourist spots. I'm quite tired, it's been day to night just walking around. It's fun, but I am really looking forward to having a day off. I'm realizing that I prefer to just chill and slow down, especially when traveling. I think that is the goal for my trips this summer. I don't need to see everything, I just want to have a good time. I think I have changed.
