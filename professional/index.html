Professional / Research

I recently graduated from Tufts University with a B.S. in Mathematics, and this August, I will be joining Columbia University as a Ph.D. student in Operations Research. During my undergraduate studies, I worked with Professor Adolfo Escobedo on Exact Linear Programming, which is the focus of my current research, and also worked with Professor James Murphy studying optimal transport theory.
